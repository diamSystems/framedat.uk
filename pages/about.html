About

framed@ is a Northern arts and music platform. Part event promoter. Part digital archive. Part tastemaker brand. We exist to document and elevate the culture happening across Manchester, Leeds, and Liverpool — the shows, the artists, the energy — before the algorithm decides it never happened.

The Mission

Build the record

Platforms change. Algorithms shift. The thing that lasts is the archive — the photos, the films, the writing. We build the owned channel that outlasts every feed.

The Work

Put on the shows

We curate and promote live events across the North of England. Independent venues. Emerging and established artists. Communities brought together by music and art.

What We Stand For

No clutter. No compromise.

Independent

We're not backed by a major label or a lifestyle brand. The work speaks. The community decides.

Northern

Manchester. Leeds. Liverpool. The culture is here — we just make sure it's seen, documented, and remembered.

Archived

Every show is photographed. Every moment documented. The archive grows with every event.

Accessible

Fair ticket prices. Real venues. Events for people who care about the music, not the status.

Behind framed@

The People

framed@ is founded and run by a small team in the North of England. We're artists, promoters, and documentarians. We believe in showing up, putting on the show, and building something that lasts.
